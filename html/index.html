Welcome to the website for spaceX fans !

SpaceX is a private American aerospace manufacturer and space transport services company founded in 2002 by Elon Musk. The company has developed the Falcon 1 and Falcon 9 launch vehicles, both designed to be reusable, and the Dragon spacecraft, which is flown into orbit by the Falcon 9 launch vehicle to supply the International Space Station (ISS) with cargo and by NASA to return cargo to Earth. SpaceX's achievements include the first privately funded liquid-propellant rocket to reach orbit (Falcon 1 in 2008), the first privately funded company to successfully launch, orbit, and recover a spacecraft (Dragon in 2010), the first private company to send and return a spacecraft from the ISS (also Dragon in 2011 and 2012), and the first propulsive landing for an orbital rocket (Falcon 9 in 2015). SpaceX has also flown 20 resupply missions to the ISS under a partnership with NASA.

As of 2021, SpaceX is contracted to launch more crewed missions to the ISS than any other company, and it has developed a super heavy-lift launch vehicle, the Starship, which it intends to use to establish a settlement on Mars.
